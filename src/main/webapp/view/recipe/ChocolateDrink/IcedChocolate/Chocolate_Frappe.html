How to Make Chocolate Frappe

A refreshing iced chocolate drink for warm days

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (240ml) milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) cold brewed coffee (optional, for mocha flavor)
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp chocolate syrup
  • 1–2 tbsp sugar or sweetener of choice
  • 1–1.5 cups ice cubes
  • Whipped cream and chocolate shavings (optional for topping)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine milk, cocoa powder, chocolate syrup, sugar, and coffee (if using).
  2. Add the ice cubes and blend on high speed until smooth and frothy.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness if necessary.
  4. Pour into a tall glass and top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings if desired.
  5. Serve immediately with a straw and enjoy the chill!

Pro Tips

  • For a thicker frappe, use frozen milk cubes instead of regular ice
  • Add a scoop of chocolate ice cream for extra richness
  • Use espresso instead of coffee for a stronger mocha kick
  • Drizzle chocolate syrup around the inside of the glass for presentation
  • Top with crushed cookies or chocolate chips for texture
