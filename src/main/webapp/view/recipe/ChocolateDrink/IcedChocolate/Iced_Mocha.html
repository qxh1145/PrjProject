How to Make Iced Mocha

A refreshing blend of rich chocolate and bold espresso over ice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strong brewed espresso or coffee, cooled
  • 1 cup milk (cold)
  • 2 tbsp chocolate syrup
  • Ice cubes
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Chocolate shavings or cocoa powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour the cooled espresso or coffee over the ice.
  3. Add the chocolate syrup and milk.
  4. Stir well to combine all ingredients.
  5. Top with whipped cream and garnish with chocolate shavings or a sprinkle of cocoa powder if desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your iced mocha!

Pro Tips

  • Use freshly brewed espresso for the best flavor.
  • Adjust the sweetness by varying the amount of chocolate syrup.
  • Try almond or oat milk for a dairy-free alternative.
  • Add a pinch of cinnamon for a warm, spicy note.
  • Blend with ice for a frappé-style iced mocha.
