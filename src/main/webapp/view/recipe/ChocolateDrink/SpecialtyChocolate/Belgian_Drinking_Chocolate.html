How to Make Belgian Drinking Chocolate

Luxuriously rich and creamy Belgian-style hot chocolate

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 4 oz high-quality Belgian dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream or chocolate shavings (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the milk in a saucepan over medium heat until warm but not boiling.
  2. Add the chopped Belgian dark chocolate, cocoa powder, and sugar.
  3. Whisk continuously until the chocolate is fully melted and the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Pour into mugs and top with whipped cream or chocolate shavings if desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy the decadent Belgian drinking chocolate experience.

Pro Tips

  • Use the best quality Belgian chocolate you can find for the richest flavor.
  • Heat gently to avoid scorching the milk or chocolate.
  • Adjust sweetness by adding more or less sugar to taste.
  • Add a pinch of cinnamon or chili powder for a subtle spicy twist.
  • Serve with a side of Belgian waffles or biscuits for a true indulgence.
